About Local SEO

Local search engine optimization is the process of making your business or company visible for location-based searches. Businesses with physical locations that serve their communities face-to-face can increase their search visibility using local SEO. These may include a departmental store, a salon, or a florist shop.

Also, it brings the service-based businesses serving in a particular geographic area like a doctor, dentist, plumber, electrician, lawyer or home cleaning company, to the kind notice of the local audience. From claiming your business listing to building citations, managing local social media engagement and online reviews, local SEO takes charge of shifting the entire focus of your local searchers towards your business.

Our Local SEO Approach

Local Search Strategy

Just like people making general searches, local searches made by them for products and services are extensive too. We do a detailed analysis of how your target audience is searching for your products and services online and create an exhaustive plan.

Update Local Business Listings

Top search engines offer an excellent opportunity to businesses to make them visible in the local searches. We make sure your business is listed on search engine pages such as Google My Business, Bing Places and more.

Dynamic Growth

Backlinks are imperative to get authority for your website which in turn helps the website rank up in search results. We get your business quality backlinks from local newspapers, business associations, local bloggers and citation sites.



Website localization

For you to be easily ‘found’ in the searches it is very important that your website should be “localized”. Localization of a website is to add specific information about the geography or market you are catering to such as the name of the city, country or region. This information should occur naturally throughout your website.

Local Business Reviews and Ratings

Searching for reviews and ratings online has become an inseparable part of the consumer journey, and just as reviews and ratings are imperative for a product they are for a local business as well. We help you with managing reviews by creating strategies that bring in positive reviews and higher ratings for your business.
